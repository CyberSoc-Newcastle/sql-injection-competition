Our Founder

John Hook founded The Geordie Boat Club in March 2022. John has been a captain of many cruise ships and yachts, with over 30 years experience. He has keen interest in boats and has collected many personal boats over the years.

Our Boats

We have an extensive collection of boats of many shapes and sizes. Our collection features the fastest UK speed boats and a very large yacht. One of the unique boats in our collection is a square spruce boat, which was donated by our VIP members Steve and Alex.

Our Events

We regularly host public and private events that have been featured in the British Boat Magazine. Our events showcase our collection of boats, and they are special occasions that are not to be missed!
